Towers of Hanoi

Tower of Hanoi is a mathematical puzzle invented in 1883 where the objective is to move a stack of discs from the first rod to the third.

There are only three rules:

  • Only one disk can be moved at a time.
  • Every move involves taking the top disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack.
  • No disk can be put on top of a smaller disk.
